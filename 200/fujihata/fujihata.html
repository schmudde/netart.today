Light on the Net project

Finally the system has been activated on 9th of June 1996

These pictures are taken on the midnight of 31th of May 1996 at the hall of Gifu Softopia Center.

Each lights are 20W bulbs. These 49 Lights are controlled by MicroChip Controller which is reading data from PC through RS-422 line. light.softopia.pref.gifu.jp is an IBM-PC/AT compatible with Pentium 90MHz and Windows 95. This PC is sending commands to MicroChip Controller, capturing video images and processing your requests.

These pictures are taken on the midnight of 31th of May 1996 at the hall of Gifu Softopia Center.
