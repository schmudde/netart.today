Transborder Immigrant Tool (2007-today)

Electronic Disturbance Theater 2.0/b.a.n.g. lab 

The Transborder Immigrant Tool was a mobile phone application intended to guide individuals who were making their way to the United States through the deserts of the U.S./Mexico borderlands to water. The application delivered poetry to its users in an effort to assist in their emotional and mental well-being while offering information about survival during the dangerous journey. ~ source
